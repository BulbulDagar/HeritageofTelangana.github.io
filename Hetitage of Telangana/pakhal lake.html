Pakhal Lake

Pakhal Lake is an artificial lake situated
in the Pakhal sanctuary close to Warangal
City in Telangana. Believed to have been
constructed in 1213 A.D by order of the Kakatiya
King Ganapathidev, the lake encompasses an
area of 30 sq km. Set around the lake is
the Pakhal Wildlife Sanctuary spread over
an area of 900 sq km.
