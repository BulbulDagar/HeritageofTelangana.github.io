Warangal

Warangal is a city in the south Indian state
of Telangana. It was the capital of the Kakatiya
dynasty from the 12th to 14th centuries,
and many intricately carved monuments from
this period still stand. Among them are the ruins
of 13th-century Warangal Fort and the Thousand
Pillar Temple, built in 1163. Set on a lake,
Bhadrakali Temple dates to the 7th century and
is dedicated to the Hindu goddess Kali.
