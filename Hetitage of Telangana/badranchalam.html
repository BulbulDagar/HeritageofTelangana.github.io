Badranchalam

Bhadrachalam is a town, known as a Hindu
pilgrimage site, in the south Indian state
of Telangana. Beside the Godavari River, the
huge 17th-century Bhadrachalam(or Sita Ramachandraswamy)
Temple complex is dedicated to Lord Rama and has many
ornate carvings. Other nearby temples include
Abhaya Anjaneya Temple, with large painted
statues of Hindu gods and goddesses.
Passenger boats travel the river to
nearby towns.
